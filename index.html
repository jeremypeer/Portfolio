The Past Meets My Future

I remember in 2012 taking classes for C++ programming and Visual Basic for my associate's degree. I ended up finishing my Bachelor's degree with a major in Accounting. I saw the need for technology and data but wasn't quite sure where those two fields intersected.

Today we know that good data and effective analysis is cruicial for making business decision. Today we have software such as Power BI and Tableau that can update information daily on the direction of a company or the progress of specific projects in real time.

• In 2020 I created a VBA Macro program for Microsoft Excel that saved our team 16 labor hours per month.

• In 2021 I started learning how to use Power BI and DAX and used it in our workplace for the first time.

• In 2022 I took an online class on SQL query language.

• In 2023 I took online classes on Tableau Dashboards, Python and R.
